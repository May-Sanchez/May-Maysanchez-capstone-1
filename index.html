About Me

My name is maria Andrea Ysabel Muzones Sanchez, Most people call me "May". I am 17 years old and I'm a student from People of Grace Christian School and I'm also a student from ZUITT. I enjoy drawing and singing. I also happens to play piano during my free time and the times where I feel stressed. I also love to write stories and novels, fanfictions, to be exact. As for now I've started my very own novel and I also insert my illustrations for it.

I started doing digital arts in January 1st of 2018, I was curious about an app I found in the playstore named "Ibis Paint X". I thought it was a coloring app, but as time goes by, I got more curious about it until I realized I started playing around its settings and finally making my first art. Year 2022 is the year I finally have my own art style, and it was satisfying to see the improvements over the years. :)
